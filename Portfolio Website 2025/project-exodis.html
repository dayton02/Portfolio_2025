Exodis Ascend

C# Unity Dialogue System Turn based System

"Exodis Ascend" is a captivating Fantasy RPG that follows your epic journey to conquer the mysterious Grand Tower Exodis. As the chosen hero, you'll face a series of challenging events that will test your wit and courage.

Every step of your ascent brings unique encounters with intriguing characters, each with their own stories and motivations. Form powerful alliances by recruiting companions into your party, each bringing distinct combat abilities that open up diverse tactical possibilities. Your choices in building your team will directly influence your combat strategy and story progression.

With its branching narrative paths and diverse character roster, each playthrough offers a fresh experience. Different character interactions, unexpected events, and various combat combinations ensure that every ascent of the tower feels like a new adventure. Will you focus on building a balanced team, or specialize in a particular combat style? The choice is yours to make.
